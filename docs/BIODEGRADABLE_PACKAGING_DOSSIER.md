° Key Distinction: Biodegradable vs Compostable
• Biodegradable: Breaks down via microbes (time undefined, may leave micro-residue)
• Compostable: Certified rapid breakdown → nutrient-rich soil (industrial/home, zero toxin)
• Mercy rule: Prefer certified compostable for closed-loop abundance

° Leading Materials 2026
• Mycelium (fungi roots) — grows in days, shock-absorbent, fully compostable
• Seaweed/Algae films — abundant ocean source, edible/dissolvable, zero land use
• PLA/PHA biopolymers — plant starch derived, industrial compostable
• Bagasse (sugarcane waste) — molded pulp, home compostable
• Fruit peel/edible coatings — zero waste, direct consumption
• Nanocellulose reinforcements — strength boost, fully biodegradable

° Innovative Examples
• Notpla — seaweed sachets (edible Ooho spheres)
